Agents & Roles

Summary

Five agents are immediately available for players to use: Brimstone, Jett, Phoenix, Sage, and Sova.

Agents can be unlocked through the following methods: one agent for 1000 Valorant Points (VP) or 8,000 Kingdom Credits (KC) in Agent Store, Kingdom Credits can only be used after Agent Recruitment Event has ended, two agents by progressing through Welcome Contract in Tiers 5 and 10, or all agents unlocked via linking to Xbox Game Pass until their subscription has ended.

Valorant Points (VP)

Kingdom Points (KP)

Each agent has 100 base HP (increased with shields by up to 50) and four abilities, including a Signature and an Ultimate ability. Depending on their abilities and playstyle, agents are assigned to one of four roles determined by the game:

  • Controllers are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.
  • Duelists are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first.
  • Initiators challenge angles by setting up their team to enter contested ground and push defenders away.
  • Sentinels are defensive experts who can lock down areas and watch flanks, both on attacker and defender rounds.
